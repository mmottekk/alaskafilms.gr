Alaska Films is a film production company in Greece, based in Athens and founded in 2013. We work across fiction, documentary and commercial film — producing original Greek projects and partnering with international producers as a co-production and service production company in Greece, including access to the EKOME cash rebate. Recent productions include Pirateland (2025), Panellinion (2024), Microbiome and End of August, with festival screenings at Clermont-Ferrand, Sarajevo, Tribeca, CPH:DOX, IndyShorts and the Thessaloniki International Film Festival.
