Films that move

Alaska Films, founded in 2013 in Athens, produces documentary and fiction films of all lengths, committed to making layered, entertaining films and bringing Greek stories to European and global audiences while fostering strong creative collaborations with directors.

We also produce commercial and branded content — including corporate films, TVCs, music videos, social media content, motion graphics and 3D animation — working with a team of experienced directors, cinematographers, editors, sound engineers and motion graphic designers.
